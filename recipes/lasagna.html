Lasagna Recipe

Description:

This is a simple lasagna dish.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • ground beef
  • 1/2 onions
  • cheese

Steps

  1. create sauce
  2. dice onions
  3. bring water to boil and put pasta in the pot
  4. boil for 10 mins
  5. In a seperate pan stir the onlines and add sauce
  6. merge pasta in the pan for 1 mins medium heat
  7. Bring the pasta on to plate and sprinkle with cheese!
